  • Game | Game of Life - Conway's Game of Life

    Conway's Game of Life is a captivating cellular automaton where simple rules govern the evolution of a dynamic ecosystem. Set on a grid, cells "live" or "die" based on their neighbors, leading to intricate patterns that evolve over time. Players observe and influence these patterns, from stable formations to chaotic behaviors, offering endless possibilities for exploration and strategy. As the grid evolves, new life forms, such as oscillators and gliders, emerge, providing both beauty and complexity to this mesmerizing simulation.

  • Game | Forest Board Game | Java

    A captivating board game scenario where participants navigate through a forest environment. Assisting ailing wildlife and collecting unique flora to enhance their medicinal capabilities, all while accruing points throughout their expedition. However, this journey is not without its perils. Along the way, players may encounter traps and encounter diverse environmental conditions, ranging from picturesque landscapes to hazardous terrains, adding depth and challenge to their experience.

  • Game | Horse Race Simulator | Java

    Dive into the excitement of competitive horse racing with this engaging Java-based Horse Race Simulator. The game simulates a thrilling race where each horse competes on its own lane, striving to cross the finish line first. Players can experience the intensity of a real horse race as they watch the horses dash forward, powered by randomized speeds and unique attributes.
