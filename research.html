Tertiary treatment of sewage

  • Research Expertise: Spearheaded comprehensive research initiatives focused on eutrophication levels in reservoirs spanning Sao Paulo's metropolitan region, collaborating closely with the Environmental Company of the State of Sao Paulo. This endeavor showcased a profound dedication to environmental sustainability, underpinned by a meticulous analysis of data and adherence to scientific methodologies. By leveraging advanced data analysis techniques and scientific principles, contributed to a deeper understanding of environmental patterns, paving the way for informed decision-making and strategic environmental conservation efforts.
  • Analytical Skills: Employed advanced statistical methods and programming techniques to analyze variables impacting the costs of implementing tertiary treatment for domestic sewage. This analysis contributed to informed decision-making regarding sewage treatment expansion, ultimately facilitating the remediation of major rivers and water bodies in the area.
  • Water Quality Assessment: Investigated trends and factors influencing water quality in reservoirs, focusing particularly on the detrimental effects of eutrophication. Evaluated the implications of these factors on the quality of water supplied to the population, contributing to enhanced environmental stewardship.
  • Operational Excellence: Achieved an outstanding 95% accuracy rate in data analysis for the tertiary treatment of domestic effluents, resulting in a forecasted cost-implementation projection of 2.27 billion US dollars for the treatment's deployment across the metropolitan region. This accomplishment underscores a dedication to operational efficiency and resource optimization, demonstrating the ability to effectively manage financial resources while ensuring the successful implementation of essential environmental initiatives.
  • Effective Communication: Skillfully communicated research findings by presenting them at Eureka, a prestigious scientific exhibition hosted annually by the college. This demonstrated proficiency in conveying complex scientific concepts to diverse audiences and highlighted strong interpersonal and presentation skills.

Stadium grandstand vibration analysis

Maua Institute of Technology | 2018

  • Comprehensive Renovation Analysis: Conducted a meticulous analysis of the renovation projects at the Allianz Park Stadium in Sao Paulo, focusing on the structural methodologies employed in expanding the grandstands. This comprehensive evaluation aimed to guarantee the integrity and safety of the stadium infrastructure while accommodating the increased seating capacity demanded by evolving spectator needs.
  • Advanced Design Techniques:Employed advanced finite element software to design a detailed model of the grandstand structure. This sophisticated tool facilitated the creation of a comprehensive digital representation, enabling precise analysis of dynamic loads arising from crowd movements and other environmental factors.
  • Structural Assessment:Conducted rigorous assessments of the grandstand's vibration modes and displacements, prioritizing the comfort and safety of spectators. This meticulous analysis aligned with industry standards and regulations, particularly crucial given the amplified dimensions of structural elements such as pillars and reinforced pre-cast beams.
  • Commitment to Safety Standards:Demonstrated a steadfast commitment to upholding safety standards and technical regulations throughout the renovation process, ensuring compliance with industry best practices. By prioritizing structural integrity and occupant safety, contributed to the creation of a secure and enjoyable environment for stadium patrons.

Flood monitoring system

Maua Institute of Technology | 2017

  • Strategic Collaboration: Partnered with both the university and the city's public administration to design the implementation of a sophisticated flood monitoring system along "Ribeirão dos Meninos" (Boy's River), a critical waterway within the city. This collaborative effort underscored a commitment to leveraging interdisciplinary expertise for effective urban water management solutions.
  • Data-Driven Analysis: Conducted an analysis to pinpoint optimal locations for sensor deployment, focusing on areas prone to high traffic and historical flooding events. This meticulous approach ensured the accuracy and reliability of water level monitoring, enabling timely interventions to mitigate flood risks and enhance public safety.
  • Innovative Development: Desgined and developed of a program interfacing with sensors to collect real-time data, including precise location coordinates and water level readings at 30-second intervals. This innovative data visualization tool facilitated the establishment of secure water level thresholds, empowering stakeholders with actionable insights to proactively manage flood events.
  • Automated Alert System: Implemented an automated alert system that seamlessly integrated historical data to deliver timely notifications to relevant stakeholders in the event of critical water level deviations. This proactive approach enabled decision-makers to respond swiftly to emerging flood risks, thereby minimizing potential damages and safeguarding community infrastructure.
