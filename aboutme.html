Hi, I am Mateus


Computer Science Student


Welcome! I am Mateus, a BSc Computer Science student within the School of Electronic Engineering and Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London.

My academic and professional journey has been shaped by a strong foundation in software development, cloud computing, data engineering, and analytics. I have worked on a wide range of projects, from optimizing ETL pipelines and managing large-scale datasets to building full-stack applications that enhance user experiences.

Through my experience with companies like Amazon AWS, Shopee, and Alelo, I have developed a deep understanding of the intersection between technology and real-world problem-solving, allowing me to create impactful solutions across various industries.

Beyond my technical expertise, I have a strong personal interest in diverse fields such as engineering, physics, and astrophysics, as well as architecture, art, and literature. I am particularly fascinated by how these disciplines intersect, whether through the mathematical principles behind structural engineering, the physics governing the universe, or the creative storytelling found in books and artistic expressions. My passion for knowledge extends to understanding broader societal concepts, from mathematical modeling in economics to the philosophical implications of technological advancements.

This blend of technical proficiency and intellectual curiosity allows me to approach challenges from a unique perspective, combining analytical thinking with creativity. I am always eager to explore new ideas, collaborate on innovative projects, and push the boundaries of what technology can achieve. Whether through developing cutting-edge software solutions or engaging with the complexities of the world around us, I strive to make a meaningful impact in everything I pursue.
